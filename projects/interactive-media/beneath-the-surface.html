LINK TO WEBSITE: Beneath the Surface

Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GSAP

Type: Interactive Scrollytelling Website

Beneath the Surface is an interactive scrolly-telling website that visualizes the journey of a discarded bottle from the shoreline to the ocean depths. Each scroll reveals pixel-art scenes, text animations and information about marine debris, transforming environmental awareness into an immersive narrative.

KEY SKILLS

  • Scrollytelling and narrative interaction design
  • GSAP ScrollTrigger
  • HTML/CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Parallax composition

The project aims to replace static infographics with smooth scroll-based storytelling. The hand-drawn pixelated art style makes the serious theme more accessible to younger audiences, while motion and particle effects help keep the ocean world alive. The site uses subtle pacing and visual rhythm to connect small actions to large consequences.
