The Disconnect is a horror-thriller animation about a grieving schoolgirl who suspects her friend's death is tied to supernatural forces. The film explores themes of identity, loneliness and fear. I was responsible for the entire sound design, except for the ending musical score.

KEY SKILLS

  • Foley and environmental texture
  • Sound layering and tonal atmosphere
  • Narrative-drive sound
  • Dialogue editing and touch up

My goal was to use sound to externalize the protagonist's growing paranoia, blending realism with psychological tension. Subtle drones, layered ambiences, and manipulated foley built a sense of unease that grows as the story unfolds. Reverb and panning were used to shape the character's shifting mental state, pulling the audience deeper into her perspective as she edges closer to danger.
