Animator: Tara Hamer

Composer: Maria Wrate

Voice Actors: Samm Eden, Rebecca Mondry-Zanaglio and Alex Cain

Audio Workstation: Ableton Live 12

The Hollow Child is a gothic, dark-fantasy short animation following a werewolf and a ghostly child in a haunting forest. I was responsible for the complete sound design and final mix, working alongside sound designer and composer Maria Wrate, who created the musical score and voice actors who performed the sounds and laughter for the ghost and the werewolf.

KEY SKILLS

  • Ableton Live 12
  • Foley, ambience creation and atmospheric layering
  • Spatial mixing
  • Dialogue editing and touch up
  • Narrative pacing
  • Mixing and Mastering

My focus was on crafting an immersive and unsettling atmosphere that complemented the animation's gothic tone. Using layered ambiences, textured winds, and distant drones, I shaped the forest into a living presence to make it both beautiful and foreboding. Through reverb, panning, and spatial mixing, I positioned sounds to guide the viewer's attention and enhance the tension between the werewolf and the ghostly child, creating a soundscape that is cohesive with the story's haunting atmosphere.
