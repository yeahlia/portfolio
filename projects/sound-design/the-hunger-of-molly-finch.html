Software: Ableton Live 12

Original game: What Remains of Edith Finch by Giant Sparrow

The Hunger of Molly Finch is a surreal, emotional short from a clip of the popular game 'What Remains of Edith Finch'. It follows Molly's descent into chaos as she transforms into a sea monster. I created the entire sound design and musical score, focusing on the gradual shift from curiosity to horror through evolving textures, spatial design and contrast.

KEY SKILLS

  • Narrative and emotional sound design
  • Foley recording and textural layering
  • Original score and synthesis
  • Emotional progression

I built the diegetic world through layered foley, underwater textures, and abstract synth tones to blur the line between realism and emotion. Drawing on synchresis, each movement was carefully tied to sound to heighten the narrative's tension. The musical score mirrors Molly's psychological descent, beginning with bright, airy melodies that gradually collapse into distorted, claustrophobic dissonance, reflecting her transformation into something monstrous.
