RED

WATCH THE FULL ANIMATION HERE!

Video credits: Jorge Jaramillo and Carlo Guillot

Audio Workstation: Ableton Live 12

The original video 'Red' is a reimagining of Little Red Riding Hood as a violent horror short, made by Jorge Jaramillo and Carlo Guillot. The film's chaotic energy and shifting perspectives offered an opportunity to design a visceral, cinematic sound world that amplifies dread and tension.

KEY SKILLS

  • Foley recording, editing and layering
  • Ambience design & environmental worldbuilding
  • Sound-image synchronization and narrative pacing
  • FX editing for horror mixing

I was responsible for the entire sound design and mix, building every foley element and atmospheric layer. My approach focused on psychological unease rather than complete realism by building anticipation through silence, drones and distorted textures. The result is a haunting immersive soundscape that mirrors the film's intensity and emotional tone.
