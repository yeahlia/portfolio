SESSION PIECES

A collection of pieces made in one session that was created to develop specific aspects of my sound design practice. Each piece focuses on a different skill, allowing me to improve and experiment freely and refine my workflow.

FOCUS AREA

  • Orchestral writing
  • Horror ambience
  • Spatial design
  • Slo-mo effects

KEY SKILLS

  • MIDI arrangement and orchestral scoring
  • Advanced automation
  • Ambience creation and atmospheric layering
  • Frequency shaping
  • Iterative practice and reflective thinking

Burst focuses a horror-based ambience and foley layering. I recorded and designed slime-based sounds, curated cinematic risers and abstract textures to build a tense and claustrophobic atmosphere, that bursts into a full frequency range. I refined my approach to depth and spatial clarity, balancing restraint and headroom to create dense mixes.

The goal for resonance was to simulate the feeling of slow motion using sound. I experimented around frequency filtering, automation and spatial mixing. I produced time stretching without using heavy time effects to explore how conventions can create the illusion of a certain sound, without muddy-ing up the mix.
