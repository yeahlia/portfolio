FIRED BEFORE HIRED

CLICK IMAGE TO PLAY THE GAME!

Developer: PRZ Games

Game Engine: Unity

Audio Workstation: Ableton and Unity

Fired Before Hired is a satirical, office-themed action game where I led the complete sound design, music composition and Unity audio implementation.

KEY SKILLS

  • Foley recording, editing and spatial placement
  • Music composition and adaptive looping structures
  • Unity Audio Managers, C# scripting and adaptive mixing
  • Mixing, mastering and in-engine integration

MENU MUSIC

BATTLE MUSIC (WAVE)

The aim was to create a humorous yet immersive experience - from the relaxing bossa nova inspired menu music, to the dynamic, intensity-based battle music layers.

I combined original foley recordings with curated sound libraries, processed everything in Ableton Live and built a responsive audio system in Unity using C# that randomized variations, adjusted real-time parameters and looped seamlessly for infinite playback.

I used a mix of my own recorded and processed foley, as well as searching and curating from existing sound libraries, and created an adaptive system in Unity that randomized variations, handled real-time audio parameters and supported seamless music looping.
